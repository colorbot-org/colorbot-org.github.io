COLORBOT

Reflection and injection do not cut it anymore. The current generation of Runescape bots is not capable of staying undetectable because they have to interact with the client to gather information about the game world. We need to find a way to bypass this limitation.

Advancements in computer vision have given us autonomous cars. These cars can model and understand our complex world from pictures alone! Colorbot uses this technology on Runescape. By taking pictures of your Runescape client, we can model the game world without ever touching the client itself. Jagex will no longer be able to ban your accounts for cheating.
